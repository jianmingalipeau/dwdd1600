Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes because Chongqing has many stairs and hills.
  • Visit Hongya Cave at night for the best views.
  • Try Chongqing hot pot, but ask for mild spice if needed.
  • Use the metro because traffic can be busy in the city center.
